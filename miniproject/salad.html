The easiest vegetable salad recipe you can make or cook. This recipe is perfect for you if you're looking for easy and healthy food that's affordable. Whether you're tired of meeting recipes or you just want food for weightloss, try this salad. Just a reminder that this contains egg if you have an egg allergy. Parmesan cheese can also be omitted if not available..

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Bunches Lettuce
  • 3 Pieces Tomatoes
  • 1/2 - 1 Piece Cucumber
  • Thousand Island Dressing
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan Cheese
